─────────────

KEY FEATURES:
─────────────
✓ No neutral midpoint (forces choice)
✓ 6 items per schema (up from 3)
✓ 18 schemas total
✓ 108 total assessment items
✓ Raw score range per item: 1-6
✓ Schema score range: 6-36 (sum of 6 items)

SCORING INTERPRETATION:
───────────────────────
Schema Mean Score (sum÷6):
• 1.0-2.0: Low endorsement
• 2.1-3.0: Mild endorsement  
• 3.1-4.0: Moderate endorsement
• 4.1-5.0: High endorsement
• 5.1-6.0: Very high endorsement

──────────────────
• < 40: Very low
• 40-49: Low
• 50-59: Average
• 60-69: Elevated (Clinical attention)
• 70+: Very elevated (Immediate attention)
